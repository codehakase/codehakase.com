# Francis Sunday
> Software engineer building reliable backend systems, with interests in distributed systems, databases, and functional programming.

## About
Francis Sunday (@codehakase) is a software engineer who works at the intersection of backend infrastructure, developer tools, and functional design. He primarily works with Go and Haskell, and cares deeply about building systems that are correct and reliable.

He currently works as a full stack engineer at [Talstack](https://talstack.com), building tools powering Africa's workforce. Previously at [Nala](https://nala.com), he contributed to the [Rafiki API](https://rafiki.com), building payment rails for remittances from Europe to Africa, integrating payout solutions across diverse African regions, and developing internal tools including a real-time transaction monitoring dashboard.

He also runs [Hakase Development Studio](https://hakasestudio.com), a small consultancy for technical founders and engineering teams building infrastructure-heavy products, focused on distributed systems, blockchain infrastructure, and long-term reliability.
